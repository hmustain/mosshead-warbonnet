Fun Facts About the Mosshead Warbonnet

"Punk Rock" Look

Some people compare the mosshead warbonnet's appearance to a "punk rock" hairstyle due to the messy, protruding cirri on its head.

Pacific Coast Inhabitant

You can primarily find the mosshead warbonnet in the temperate waters of the North Pacific Ocean, particularly along the coast of California.

Small Size

These fish are relatively small, typically only growing up to around 6 inches long. That's the size of a One Dollar Bill!
